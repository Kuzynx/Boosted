Our Story

The Mission

Boosted was born from a simple frustration: automotive culture is thriving, but the information connecting us is scattered across dead forums and social media noise.

We set out to build a centralized digital heartbeat for the community. A place where every enthusiast—from JDM purists to American Muscle collectors—can find their crew and fuel their passion without the static.

Community First

We build tools specifically designed for the way car enthusiasts interact, organize, and host events.

Modern Access

No more digging through old threads. Real-time maps and instant notifications keep you in the loop.

Inclusive Culture

Whether it's a local coffee meet or a massive rally, Boosted is home for everything on wheels.
