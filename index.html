FAQ

How much does Boosted cost?

Boosted is free to download on the App Store and Google Play. Create your garage, find meets, and join crews at no cost.

Which devices are supported?

Boosted is available for both iOS and Android. Download it from the Apple App Store or Google Play.

How do I host my own meet?

Open the app, head to Events, and tap create. You can set the location, time, and details, then share it with the community.

How do I get support?

Reach our team anytime at our contact page — email or phone, typically answered within 24 hours.
